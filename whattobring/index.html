What to bring to the biking tour.

9 things you will need to bring to the biking tour.

Sunglasses

Bring a good pair of sunglasses to avoid glare on the trail.

Entrance Fee

Please dont forget to bring 53 Bolivianos as entrance fee for the communities that live around the Death Road.

Sunscreen

Don't get a sunburn, bring a sunscreen with you to the trip.

Swimming Attire

If you plan to relax at the pool or swim, we advice you to bring a swimming attire and don't forget your flip flops.

Extra Shoes and Clothes

Down the road you will encounter with waterfalls and mud, so please dont forget to bring an extra pair of shoes and socks to change after the ride.

Insect repellent

Nothing more annoying than mosquitoes, so please don't forget to bring insect repellent to the trip.

Warm Clothes and Raincoat

At the beginning of the ride it gets colder and can be snowing, so please don't forget to bring warm clothes and a raincoat.

Extra Cash

After the ride, you'll have some time to grab food and drinks, so if you want to enjoy a beer or buy an extra bottle of water, please bring some extra cash on you to the tour.

Personal Hygiene Products

Please, don't forget to bring all personal hygiene products such as towel, soap, shampoo, and any other products you require to have a refreshing shower at the end of the ride.
